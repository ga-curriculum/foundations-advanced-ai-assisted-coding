## About

In this hands-on lab, you’ll move from using generic AI tools to building a custom GPT designed to guide you through complex coding and data planning tasks. You’ll compare different model types, create your own AI assistant for pseudocoding, and put it to the test on challenging real-world scenarios. By the end, you’ll know how to design, build, and iterate on an AI partner that fits your workflow.

## Content

| Lesson | Est. Delivery Time | Skills |
| ------ |:------------------:| ------ |
| [Types of Models, Custom GPTs, and Their Capabilities](./microlesson-01/README.md) | ~20 min | Distinguish between general models, specialized models, and custom GPTs; identify strengths and limitations. |
| [Build Your Custom Pseudocode GPT](./microlesson-02/README.md) | ~60 min | Design, build, and refine a custom GPT for planning and pseudocoding complex problems. |
| [Solve a Coding Problem With Your Custom GPT](./microlesson-03/README.md) | ~40 min | Use your assistant to tackle complex, real-world logic and data scenarios; reflect and iterate. |
| **Total content**                                   | 120 min | Apply AI model knowledge to create and use custom AI coding assistants. |


### Prerequisites

- Experience using ChatGPT (or equivalent)
- Basic understanding of pseudocode and coding workflows
- Access to ChatGPT’s custom GPT builder

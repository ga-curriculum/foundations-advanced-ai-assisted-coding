Now it’s your turn! You’ll design, build, and refine your very own custom GPT to help with pseudocoding and workflow planning.

---

## Step 1: Open the GPT Builder

1. In ChatGPT, click **Explore GPTs** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Create** (or **+ New GPT**).
3. Give your GPT a name, like `Pseudocode Coach` or `Data Workflow Buddy`.

---

## Step 2: Add Instructions and Conversation Starters

- **Instructions:** What should your GPT always do?  
  Example:  
  - “Always ask clarifying questions before generating pseudocode.”
  - “Never give runnable code—only step-by-step logic.”
  - “Prompt for edge cases or missing info.”
  - “Output must be formatted as plain text pseudocode.”

- **Conversation starters:** Add a few example prompts to get things rolling, like:
  - “Help me plan a function to process customer orders.”
  - “Guide me in outlining logic for cleaning messy data.”
  - “Ask me for all requirements before writing any pseudocode.”

---

## Step 3: Test and Iterate

- Try out your custom GPT with a real pseudocoding prompt.
- What works? What needs to be changed or clarified?
- Edit instructions, test again, and keep refining.
- **Bonus:** Ask a partner to try your GPT and give you feedback.

---

## Step 4: Share and Celebrate

- Share your favorite feature or best prompt with the group.
- What did you learn from the process?
- Would you use your custom GPT in real projects?

> 💡 Remember: Your assistant is only as strong as your instructions.  
> The more you clarify, the better the results.
